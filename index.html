Airbnb Data in New York City

Airbnb is a well-known platform that focuses on short-term stays, connecting people who want to rent out their homes with people who wants to stay in an accommodations in specific area for a short period of time. This is a simple visualization that allows viewer to know the general information about Airbnb in New York City. Click on each borough to highlight their information.

This visualisation is created by jennyvanlinh. The datasource and map data are from Airbnb Open Data and NYC OpenData.
